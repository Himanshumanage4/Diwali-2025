Wishing You a Joyful Diwali!

Diwali, the festival of lights, is celebrated to honor the victory of light over darkness, good over evil, and knowledge over ignorance. Families decorate their homes with diyas, candles, and rangoli, share sweets, and enjoy fireworks to mark the joyous occasion.
